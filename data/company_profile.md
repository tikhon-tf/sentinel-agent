# Meridian Health Technologies, Inc.

## Company Overview

**Founded:** 2018  
**Headquarters:** Boston, Massachusetts, USA  
**Global Offices:** London (UK), Berlin (Germany), Singapore, Toronto (Canada)  
**Employees:** ~2,400  
**Annual Revenue:** ~$380M (FY2025)  

Meridian Health Technologies is an AI-powered healthcare fintech company that combines advanced machine learning with healthcare domain expertise and financial services infrastructure. The company serves healthcare providers, payers, and patients across North America and the European Union.

## Business Lines

### 1. Clinical AI Platform
AI-driven clinical decision support tools, diagnostic imaging analysis, patient risk scoring, and adverse event prediction systems. Deployed in 340+ hospitals and clinics across the US and EU. Classified as high-risk AI under EU AI Act (Annex III, Section 5 — AI systems intended to be used as safety components in the management and operation of critical digital infrastructure, road traffic, or in the supply of water, gas, heating, or electricity).

**Key Products:**
- MeridianDx — Diagnostic imaging AI (FDA 510(k) cleared, CE marked)
- CarePredict — Patient deterioration early warning system
- RxGuard — Drug interaction and contraindication checking
- PopHealth AI — Population health risk stratification

### 2. HealthPay Financial Services
Healthcare-specific payment processing, patient financing, medical lending, and claims automation. Processes approximately $4.2B in transactions annually. Subject to federal banking regulations, SR 11-7 model risk management guidance, and SOC 2 requirements.

**Key Products:**
- HealthPay Processing — Medical payment gateway
- PatientFin — Patient financing and medical lending
- ClaimFlow — AI-powered claims adjudication
- FraudShield — Real-time fraud detection for healthcare payments

### 3. MedInsight Analytics
Population health analytics, care gap identification, outcomes prediction, and quality measure reporting platform for health systems and insurers. Handles protected health information (PHI) from approximately 12 million patients.

### 4. Meridian SaaS Platform
Cloud-based infrastructure underlying all products. Multi-tenant architecture hosted on AWS (us-east-1, eu-west-1) with Azure disaster recovery. SOC 2 Type II certified since 2021.

## Organizational Structure

Meridian operates through 10 business units, each maintaining their own Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) aligned with the company's regulatory obligations:

1. **AI/ML Engineering** — ML model development, training, deployment, monitoring
2. **Clinical AI Products** — Clinical validation, regulatory submissions, post-market surveillance
3. **Data Governance & Privacy** — Data classification, privacy impact assessments, data subject rights
4. **Financial Services** — Payment processing, lending, fraud detection, regulatory reporting
5. **Information Security** — Access control, vulnerability management, incident response
6. **IT Operations & Infrastructure** — Change management, disaster recovery, service management
7. **Human Resources** — Employee lifecycle, training, workplace policies
8. **Legal & Compliance** — Regulatory affairs, internal audit, policy management
9. **Product & Engineering** — SDLC, code quality, API standards, platform reliability
10. **Customer Operations** — Customer support, data handling, SLA management
